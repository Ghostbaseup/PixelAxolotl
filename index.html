Axolotl

The axolotl, Ambystoma mexicanum, is a type of neotenic salamander related to the tiger salamander. They are known for their ability to regenerate limbs, gills, and parts of their hearts and brains. Unlike most other salamanders, axolotls retain their larval features throughout their life, including their external gills and aquatic lifestyle.
